House Rules

  • Bingo = any complete row, column, or diagonal (5 in a row). The center FREE DAY is yours to claim once each — use it on a real rest day where you let your body recover (sleep in, light stretch, no guilt).
  • Verification: Honor-system check-ins. Show your Oura app screenshot if challenged.
  • Why these squares: daily step goal stays at 10k as a baseline (no square reward — that's the floor). Stretch days, sleep streaks, strength sessions, and food/drink habits are where the real progress lives for weight loss.
  • Tie-breaker on May 31: highest combined Activity Score for the month wins. Still tied? Co-plan the trip.
  • Click a square to log the date you completed it. Click an already-logged square to edit the date or clear it. Names and progress save automatically in your browser.
